## How Content Is Optimized for SEO

Each AI-generated article is prompted to include:
- Keyword-rich title (SEO-friendly)
- 4-6 H2 headers with search terms people use
- 900-1200 word length (optimal for Google ranking)
- Natural keyword placement in the first paragraph
- Specific examples, actionable advice, and data points
- Extracted tags (6 keywords) stored in frontmatter
- 140-160 character meta description (excerpt)

## How to Monitor SEO Performance

### Google Search Console

Check weekly:
1. Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Select `succeedstack.com`
3. Click **Performance** → see clicks, impressions, average position
4. Click **Pages** → see which pages are indexed
5. Click **Core web vitals** → check page speed scores

### What to Look For

- **Impressions increasing** → Google is showing your pages in results
- **Clicks increasing** → Users are clicking your results
- **Average position improving** → Rankings moving up (lower number = better)
- **Pages indexed** should grow as new posts are published

## Tips to Improve Rankings

### Short Term

- Ensure all pages load fast (Vercel + CDN handles this)
- Make sure sitemap is always up to date (it is — auto-generated)
- Keep publishing regularly (GitHub Actions handles this)

### Medium Term

- Add internal links between related articles
- Make article titles more specific and question-based (e.g., "How to..." "What is...")
- Finance and crypto topics tend to rank faster due to high search volume

### Long Term

- Build backlinks by sharing articles on Reddit, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Submit individual articles to social bookmarking sites
- Consider adding a newsletter to build return visitors
