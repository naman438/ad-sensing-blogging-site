There are several types of **cross-chain bridges**, including **trusted** and **trustless** bridges. **Trusted** bridges rely on a centralized intermediary to facilitate the exchange of assets, while **trustless** bridges use **smart contracts** to enable the transfer of assets in a **decentralized** manner. **Trustless** bridges are generally considered to be more **secure** and **scalable** than **trusted** bridges, as they do not rely on a single, centralized point of failure.
